About Dream Steals Warehouse

Welcome to Dream Steals Warehouse, where you can score incredible deals on bedding and appliances!

We work in a warehouse that handles logistics for multiple retailers. Sometimes, items get returned or refused by customers for reasons like a small scratch or torn packaging.

We get these almost-new (and sometimes totally new) items and sell them at a fraction of the regular market price. You get high-quality merchandise at a great price, and we help reduce waste.

We don't usually allow customers in the warehouse, except on Saturdays if you want to check out what we have in person, you can give us a call or contact our marketplace representative.

As for mattresses and boxspring deals, delivery is included in the price.
